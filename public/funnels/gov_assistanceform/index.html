Frontend Service Sections

This public-facing interface lets applicants select the assistance type, submit their information, upload requirements, and track their case reference online.

S

Scholarship

Collect student details, school information, enrollment proof, grades, and education support requirements.

L

Livelihood

Capture livelihood requests, small business details, proposal data, and community validation requirements.

M

Medical

Submit patient records, diagnosis information, hospital documents, and billing support requirements.

B

Burial

Provide claimant details, deceased information, funeral documents, and assistance release records.

Applicant Journey

Simple process for the public while keeping the internal handling structured for the government office.

1

Select Assistance Type

Applicants choose scholarship, livelihood, medical, or burial assistance.

2

Fill Out Digital Form

Citizen enters details and uploads the required documents online.

3

Receive Reference Number

System generates a case reference and sends confirmation by SMS or email.

4

Track Application Status

Applicant can monitor if the request is pending, incomplete, approved, or released.
