FAQ

Answers before you book.

Common questions from clients new to aesthetic care at Neo Pali.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

+

Yes. A consultation helps determine the most suitable treatment based on your goals, skin condition, and wellness needs. It's also free for first-time visitors.

Are treatments safe?

+

All treatments at Neo Pali are guided by a licensed medical aesthetic doctor and follow safe clinical protocols to ensure the best experience and results.

How do I book an appointment?

+

You may call or text 0992-426-8477 / 0997-9911-182, fill out the form on this page, or visit the clinic directly at Pricilla Bldg., Cabanatuan City.

Do you accept walk-in clients?

+

Walk-ins are welcome, though booking ahead is recommended to ensure your preferred schedule and minimize waiting time at the clinic.

What skin concerns do you treat?

+

We address acne, pigmentation, aging, whitening, body slimming, mole/wart removal, and more. Visit us and our doctor will create a personalized plan just for you.

Do you sell skincare products?

+

Yes! We carry curated skincare products including serums, sunscreens SPF 50+, Glutathione capsules, collagen drinks, and acne control kits — all available at the clinic.
